A car or an automobile is a motor vehicle with wheels. Most definitions of cars say that they run primarily on roads, seat one to eight people, have four wheels, and mainly transport people, not cargo.

The Lotus Evora GT is the only model sold by the British automaker in the U.S. Two seating configurations are available: a 2+2 with tiny rear jump seats or a two-seat layout. As with past Lotus models, the Evora GT combines extensive weight reduction measures with a powerful engine, responsive chassis, and improved aerodynamics to create a visceral driving experience. With the available Carbon pack, the Evora GT weighs just over 3,100 pounds with the standard six-speed manual transmission or about 25 pounds more with the available six-speed automatic.
